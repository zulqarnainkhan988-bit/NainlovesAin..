A small letter

💌
tap the envelope to open it

Ain,

I don't think I've ever told you, in so many words, just how much room you take up in my life — in the best way. You're not a small part of my days, you're the part everything else gets measured against.

You're beautiful in the way that catches me off guard even now — not just how you look, though that's true too, but in the way you laugh without holding back, the way your face gives away exactly what you're feeling, the way you carry yourself like you don't even know how much light you bring into a room. I notice it every time.

But it's everything underneath that I love even more. The way you care about people. The way you think things through. The way you're soft and strong in exactly the right places, at exactly the right times. You make ordinary moments feel like they matter, just by being in them.

You're important to me in a way I don't have great words for — the kind of important where my plans, my mood, my whole sense of things going well, all bend a little around you. I built this whole silly site just to say that properly, for once, instead of in passing.

Happy birthday, Ain. I love you.

— Nain
